Elderly-Oriented Interior Design and Planning Guidlines

Project information

  • Category: Landscape Design
  • Client: Zhongnan Holding Group Co., Ltd.
  • Project date: August, 2014
  • Project Scale: Unknown

This research, from the perspective of planning and designing as the starting point probes into the practical problems encountered in the designing of a new type of living with the background of an aging society, providing a reference for the mode of elderly-home-living product and proposing a new direction for the specializedelderly-living environment, In combination with the real estate projects of scientific research for Zhongnan Construction Group Co., Ltd., we selected a proper zone in the Gulf of Tangshan, the original ordinary residential area, for suitable reconstruction and planning of aging via researching apartment layout of elderly included functional layout traffic, landscape environment and other aspects, in order to meet the requirements of elderly for living under the model of aging settlements. The research in this text on the design of new department layouts for elderly-living-home has more far-reaching practical significance, although it is in a preliminary exploration stage.
