Urban Design of Shizikou Area, Changshan County

Project information

  • Category: Urban Design
  • Client: Changshan County Natural Resources and Planning Bureau
  • Project date: October, 2020
  • Project Scale: 184.42 ha

The urban design project in the Shizikou area of Changshan County centers around the concept of a "Landscape Park City," emphasizing the role of ecological civilization in guiding urban development. This project is people-centric and aims to establish a symbiotic community of mountains, forests, skies, and lakes, achieving a harmonious unity between humans and nature, the city and its environment, and industrial development. Key features include universally accessible landscaped and garden-like settings, enhanced ecological protection, and promotion of biodiversity and livability. Additionally, the project focuses on expanding green infrastructure and fostering economic innovation to create a convenient and beautiful comprehensive service center.
