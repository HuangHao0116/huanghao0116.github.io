Jiangshan City South Entrance Node Enhancement and Improvement

Project information

  • Category: Landscape Design
  • Client: Jianshan City Government
  • Project date: August, 2020
  • Project Scale: 40700 m2

This project aims to enhance urban greening and landscaping in Jiangshan City. By revitalizing the greenery of traffic islands, medians, and sidewalks, the project emphasizes natural ecology and urban charm, creating a cityscape that blends historical depth with modernity, akin to an emerald necklace. The design prioritizes ecological sustainability, combining terrain modification and plant diversity to elevate the city's gateway image and the integrated use of functional areas, thereby enhancing urban vitality and cultural identity.
