The Concept Planning and Architectural Design of Xingtai High-tech Industrial Development Zones

Project information

  • Category: Architecture Design
  • Project date: December, 2014
  • Project Scale: 14.93 ha

This project blends Republican-era style with modern design to create an office space that meets contemporary functional needs. The architecture uses traditional stone materials, is dignified and symmetrical, showcasing a fusion of Eastern and Western architectural art. The interior is open, featuring traditional decorations and embodying the "philosophy of square and circle," aiming to create a workspace that is both traditional and modern.
