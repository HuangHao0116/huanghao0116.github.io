Planning and Architectural Design of Taizhou Cultural and Creative Industry Clusters

Project information

  • Category: Urban Design and Architecture Design
  • Client: Zhejiang Taizhou Economic Development Zone Management Committee (Taizhou Green Heart Tourism Resort Management Committee)
  • Project date: November, 2015
  • Project Scale: 121 ha

The planning and architectural design project of Taizhou Cultural and Creative Industry Cluster is based on the concepts of "seed breeding" and "organic renewal". Under the development model of platform support, industrial cluster, and functional compounding, it creates a park integrating intelligent manufacturing, internet+, cultural creativity, entrepreneurship and innovation, and collaborative services. Through multi-industry integration and innovative development, coordinating industry and urban construction, and developing industrial cultural tourism, the project organically renews and reshapes spaces to create gateway spaces. The plan constructs city roads with diverse and heterogeneous characteristics, safe and convenient pedestrian streets, an open space system of double-loop linked pocket parks, and a natural ecological network combining corridors and patches.
