Zhongjian Minle Residential District

Project information

  • Category: Urban Design
  • Client: China Construction Eighth Engineering Division. Corp. LTD
  • Project date: June, 2014
  • Project Scale: 599.39 ha

The project draws inspiration from Howard's Garden City theory, British New Towns, and New Urbanism to construct a theme-based ecological central park, a slow-traffic system network, and a transit-oriented community, forming a vibrant and diverse living space that blends modern planning principles with the local cultural and ecological context. The project revolves around the theme of "peach blossoms," integrating residential areas of Minle Residential District with the Central Peach Blossom Park and the transit-oriented commercial complex through urban roads and ecological corridors, which aims to make Minyue City a unique and desirable destination in Huinan Town, Pudong New Area, and even Shanghai, known as "Minle Peach Blossom Utopia City."
